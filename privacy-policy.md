Privacy Policy — Vela Apps
Last updated: March 2026
All apps published by Patchmedia  ("the Apps") are operated as independent mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.
Data Storage
All data you enter — including period dates, cycle length, symptoms, moods, and notes — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal health data on any server. We have no access to your data.
No Account Required
The Apps do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information to use any feature.
Advertising
The Apps display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
Data We Do Not Collect
We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, camera, or any health data. The only information that leaves your device is standard advertising identifiers used by AdMob, which we do not control or access.
Children's Privacy
The Apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
